## まま
as it is; current state; without changing~
- Verb (た Form / ない Form) + まま
- Noun + の + まま
- な-adjective + な + まま
- い-adjective + まま

## っぱなし
leaving (something) on; leaving (something) still in use​
- Verb (Stem From) + っぱなし
